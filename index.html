    Who we are?

    Our team helps students at every level to learn Math and Science concepts easily by teaching everything from absolute basics to make them understand the concepts better and identify where the students are lagging at, will help schools and parents to identify individual students learning gap.

    What we offer ?

    Learning / Testing

    Have you ever thought of having a teacher, who teaches you all the concepts from basics at your place and your convenient time. In Learn Basics students can learn all the concepts from the best minds of the country.

    Gap Identification

    Take tests which can let you know where the students are lagging at, identifying the weak areas at concept, topic and chapter level from individual students to school level. This will helps the teacher to bridge the gap between studnets and concepts indudually and will upload the teacher burden.

    Communication

    teachers will recieve their class students' performance report in their email inbox / whatsapp once in a fortnight, for daily update teachers and principals can use our Learn Basics - Insights app.
